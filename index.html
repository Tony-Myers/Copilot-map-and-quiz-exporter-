Copilot Study Tool Exporter

Convert Copilot-generated study materials into quizzes, interactive mind maps, and podcast-style audio

How this works

Step 1 — Generate study material in Copilot

Use the Copilot study assistant to create either a quiz or a mind map from your article, chapter, or notes.

Step 2 — Paste the Copilot response

Copy the full Copilot response and paste it into the box below, or use Paste from Clipboard.

Step 3 — Open the study tool

After saving the payload, open either the Quiz Tool or Mind Map Tool to study, edit, or export the material.

Optional — Podcast-style audio

You can also paste a short Host / Expert dialogue script and play it aloud with two browser voices.
