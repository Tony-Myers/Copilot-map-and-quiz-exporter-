Copilot Study Tool Exporter

Convert Copilot-generated study materials into quizzes and interactive mind maps

How this works

Step 1 — Generate study material in Copilot

Use the Copilot study assistant to create either a quiz or a mind map from your article or notes.

When Copilot finishes, it will provide a link to open this tool automatically.

Step 2 — Paste the Copilot response

Copy the full Copilot response and paste it into the box below. The tool will automatically detect and extract the JSON study data.

Step 3 — Open the study tool

After saving the payload, open either the Quiz Tool or Mind Map Tool to study, edit, or export the material.
